About WUMU

WUMU is the brain child of James Kearl. After years of designing mass production furniture and overseeing large manufacturing ventures, he decided to return to his main passion- designing and creating beautiful furniture. Originally from the Bay Area, CA he recently relocated to Utah Valley for the express purpose of making awesome stuff. He is a third generation wood worker programmed to make your furniture dreams come true.

WUMU will take you through the complete design process to help create your own take on perfect furniture. We can execute the idea you've already thought up or we can guide you through the design process to arrive at exactly what you always wanted, but couldn't figure out how to express. This process is most effective when we sit down together in your home, in the space you are designing for. That way we know the final destination of our work exactly. Walking you through ideas, photos, Pinterest or Houzz boards, we can translate what you've liked into what you'll love. See our Contact page and get in touch.
